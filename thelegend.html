MY EXPERIENCE

Most recently, I managed Accounts Payable at a super hip digital marketing agency. Tell me, do you know what it's like working at a cool company doing the least cool job imaginable? It decimates the soul. I quit. I want to make stuff.

I did some other stuff over the course of my long, 6-year life. I spent some time in the Caribbean, weaving baskets. I played a wood elf at a theme park. I counted votes for Gore in Florida, maybe poorly, I don't think he did very well.

MY SCHOOLING

I went to UoC. Not California, although you'd be forgiven for getting confused. I went to the majestic, world-renowned, University of Calgary. I studied accounting at the Haskayne School of Business. I regret it.
